Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Do I need a prescription for these treatments?

Yes, all our treatments require a prescription. Our licensed healthcare providers will evaluate your health profile and determine if these treatments are appropriate for you during the consultation process.

How quickly will I receive my medications?

Once your treatment plan is approved, we typically ship within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary by location but most patients receive their medications within 3-5 business days.

Are these treatments covered by insurance?

Our services are designed to be affordable without insurance. We offer transparent pricing and payment plans to make these treatments accessible. Some insurance plans may provide partial reimbursement - we can provide documentation for you to submit.

Is my personal health information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-leading encryption and security measures to protect your personal health information. Our platform is HIPAA compliant and we never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.
